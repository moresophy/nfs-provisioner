# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as maintainers and contributors of NFS-PROVISIONER pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at sebastian.broers@moresophy.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
